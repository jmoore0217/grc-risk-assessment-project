# Risk Assessment Report

## Executive Summary
This risk assessment identified key information security and operational risks affecting the organization.

## Top Risks
1. No control or governance for thumb drive usage or storage
2. No documented policies or procedures
3. Inconsistency of documentation for sanitization

## Risk Treatment Summary
High risks require immediate mitigation through access controls, monitoring, and policy enforcement.

## Conclusion
The organization should prioritize security controls and compliance monitoring to reduce overall risk exposure.

# ⚠️ Risk Assessment Methodology

## Risk Identification
Risks were identified through:
- Review of business processes
- Common industry threats


## Risk Scoring
Each risk was scored using:
- Likelihood: 0 (Low) to 10 (High)
- Impact: 0 (Low) to 10 (High)

Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact
